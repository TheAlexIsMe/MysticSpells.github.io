Mystic Spells Shop Rules
To keep our coven cozy, magical, and drama‑free:
  • Always RP in character — no OOC in the shop.
  • No drama — leave conflicts outside. We are here for magic, not mess.

  • Respect everyone — no bias, no exclusion. All witches and visitors are welcome.
  • No guns — leave firearms outside. This is a safe magical space.
  • Keep it magical — Mystic Spells is a place to hang, chill, and enjoy.
